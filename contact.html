Frequently Asked Questions

  • What kind of camera am I using?

    All of the photos on this page were taken with Nikon Cameras. My first DSLR was a Nikon D5100 and I am currently using a Nikon D5500.

  • What lenses am I using?

    When backpacking, I only carry one lens. I currently have a Tamron 16-300mm which I carried on my 2018 trips. Prior to 2017, I used a Sigma 18-250mm lens.

  • How do I carry my camera?

    I use a Peak Design Capture clip on my backpack strap. I absolutely love it. My camera is always handy. For more information about the clip, please visit Peak Design - Camera Clips.

  • How did I keep my camera dry on the trail?

    Pretty much nothing stays completely dry on the Appalachian Trail, but I managed to keep my camera mostly dry with a simple light weight dry sack. I also carried a gallon ziplock freezer bag as extra protection in the torrential rains. My other piece of gear to help with condensation and humidity was the mixed silca gel dessicant packets.

  • Do I have any more trips planned?

    I am currently working and going to school. Once I have graduated, I do plan on returning to the Appalachian Trail. I am not sure if I try to thru-hike again or if I will just finish my remaining 500 miles.
