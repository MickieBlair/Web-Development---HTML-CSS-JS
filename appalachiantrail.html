Appalachian Trail

From Georgia to Maine, the Appalachian Trail holds the most wonderful sites. Over the last few years, I have walked around 2500 miles. While I haven't seen every mile at least once, I plan on continuing that adventure at every opportunity. The trail is marked by white blazes and I hope to see them all .
